FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the rough price of developing a mobile application with TechGropse?

Mobile app pricing varies based on the complexity, features, the platform (iOS, Android, or both), and design needs. The cost to develop a basic application can be as low as approximately $5,000, and a fully-fledged business application can be very expensive.

Does TechGropse have any after-sales services?

In our opinion, your app is only the start. TechGropse has full after-sales services such as bug corrections, performance and compatibility with OS improvement, and addition of features.

Is there a Non-Disclosure Agreement signed by TechGropse?

Absolutely. The privacy and intellectual property matters to you are of the highest priority. We do not mind signing an NDA prior to the project discussions.

What is the duration of developing a mobile app?

The timeline of development will be based on the scale, complexity, and characteristics of the app. MVP can be implemented in 4-8 weeks, and a full-fledged, tailor-made app can take between 3 and 9 months.

What technologies and platforms are adopted by TechGropse?

Our areas of expertise are native iOS (Swift), native Android (Kotlin), and cross-platform platforms, such as React native and Flutter. We will choose the optimal technology stack depending on your business objectives, finances, and audience.

Which is superior, iOS or Android mobile apps?

It relies on who your target audience is. In case the majority of users are Android, target them; in case they are iOS, target Apple. To reach as many people as possible, a cross-platform application on both platforms is the best.
